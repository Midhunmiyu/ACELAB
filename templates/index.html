Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I schedule an appointment?

    You can schedule an appointment by calling our hospital at [Phone Number] or by using our online appointment request form. We will do our best to accommodate your preferred date and time.

  • What if I have a medical emergency?

    If you have a medical emergency, please call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room. If you are unsure whether your situation constitutes a medical emergency, please contact our hospital or your primary care physician for guidance.

  • What insurance plans do you accept?

    We accept most major insurance plans, including star health and LIC. Please contact your insurance provider or our billing department to verify coverage.

  • What should I bring with me for my appointment or hospital stay

    Please bring your ID, insurance card, and any relevant medical records or test results. Depending on the nature of your visit, you may also need to bring personal items such as toiletries or comfortable clothing. Our staff will provide you with a detailed list of what to bring prior to your appointment or hospital stay.

  • How can I access my medical records?

    You can access your medical records through our patient portal. If you have not yet signed up for the patient portal, please contact our medical records department for assistance.
